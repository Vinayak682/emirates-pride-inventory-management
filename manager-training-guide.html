Overview

As a Manager, you have comprehensive access to the Emirates Pride Stock Register system across all regional divisions. Your role requires monitoring inventory, managing store operations, reviewing tester assignments, and overseeing daily sales performance across all locations.

Manager Responsibilities

  • Monitor daily stock levels across all stores
  • Review and approve store transfers
  • Manage tester bottle inventory and write-offs
  • Lock/unlock daily records for data integrity
  • Access demand planning and benchmarks
  • Review financial records and cash/card/BNPL entries
  • Oversee Area Manager assignments and store access
  • Generate reports for executive review

Key Manager Features

📊 Dashboard

Real-time KPI cards showing total units sold, active stores, top performers, and activity logs. Access from the top bar.

🏪 All Stores View

Compare stock levels across all stores in a single scrollable panel. Switch between regions and days seamlessly.

🔒 Lock System

Lock any day's data to prevent accidental edits. Only Managers and the data owner can unlock. Timestamp logged for audit.

🧴 Tester Management

Track tester bottles by condition (Active/Low/Empty/Sealed). View write-offs and issuances per store.

💰 Finance Overview

Review cash, card, and BNPL entries. Audit daily finance records and identify discrepancies.

📁 Demand Planning

Access benchmark data, reorder priorities, and SKU performance metrics. Identify slow movers and reorder alerts.

Best Practices

Daily Manager Checklist

Log in to Stock Register every morning to review overnight inventory changes
Check All-Stores panel to identify low-stock or problem areas
Review new transfers and verify they are correctly entered
Lock the previous day's records to ensure data integrity
Monitor tester inventory for low/empty bottles and plan replenishment
Review finance entries for accuracy and resolve discrepancies
Check the Dashboard for top/bottom performers and unusual trends
Always log out at the end of your shift

Important Notes

⚠ Data Integrity

Once a day is locked, no store-level edits are permitted. Only Managers can unlock. This ensures historical data remains accurate for audits and reports. If a store needs to edit a locked day, contact support for a Manager unlock.

Support & Escalation

  • Technical Issues: Contact the IT team immediately
  • Data Discrepancies: Review the activity log for who made changes and when
  • Access Issues: Verify PIN and store selection before retrying
  • Emergency Unlock: Contact System Administrator for urgent unlock requests
