Core Functions

Key Features

الميزات الرئيسية

1. Dashboard View

عرض لوحة التحكم

After logging in with PIN 4000, your dashboard automatically filters to show only your 6 stores. You'll see:

  • Daily sales summary by store
  • Northern Emirates totals and KPIs
  • Activity log of all store operations

2. Store Selection

اختيار المتجر

The store dropdown in the app shows only your assigned stores. You cannot view or edit stores managed by other Area Managers.

3. Day Management

إدارة الأيام

Once all daily entries are complete:

  • Click the Lock icon to prevent edits
  • Locked days appear with a padlock symbol
  • Only you can unlock (with PIN 4000)

4. Export & Reporting

التصدير والتقارير

Generate region-wide reports in multiple formats:

  • CSV — for spreadsheet analysis
  • Excel — with formatting & formulas
  • PDF — for printing & sharing

5. Demand Planning

تخطيط الطلب

Access SKU benchmarks and reorder priorities:

  • Read-only view of demand analytics
  • Helps guide inventory orders
  • Northern Emirates insights

6. Finance Tab

علامة التمويل

Track daily payments across your stores:

  • Cash collections
  • Card transactions
  • BNPL (Tabby/Tamara) payments
Operations

Best Practices

أفضل الممارسات
📋 Daily Checklist
قائمة التحقق اليومية
  1. Morning: Review overnight activity, check all store status lights
  2. Mid-day: Spot-check a few stores for data accuracy
  3. Evening: Ensure all stores have submitted data by cutoff
  4. End-of-day: Review regional totals, lock completed days
⚠️ Important Notes
ملاحظات مهمة
  • Never share your PIN (4000) with store staff
  • Store staff use their own individual store PINs (shown above)
  • If you forget your PIN, contact the Demand Planning team
  • Locked days cannot be edited without unlocking them first
  • Always verify store totals match your mental math before locking
🔗 Support & Escalation
الدعم والتصعيد

If you encounter issues:

  • App login problems: Verify PIN (4000) is entered correctly
  • Store data discrepancies: Check with the store manager before locking
  • Technical bugs: Screenshot the issue and email support team
  • Access questions: Contact regional operations manager
