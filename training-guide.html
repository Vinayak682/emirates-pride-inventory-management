01
What is the Stock Register? ما هو سجل المخزون؟

The Emirates Pride Stock Register is the official daily inventory tracking system used by every store in the UAE, Oman, and KSA. It records every product movement — what came in, what was sold, what was transferred — and calculates the live closing balance for each SKU every day.

📱
The register is a web app — it runs in the browser on any phone, tablet, or PC. No app download needed. Bookmark the link: vinayak682.github.io/emirates-pride-inventory-management/stock-register.html
🔄
All data is saved automatically to the cloud (Supabase server). If your phone restarts or you close the browser, your entries are safe. The manager can see all stores' data in real time.

Understanding the Grid فهم الجدول

The grid shows every product in your store on the left, and 8 columns of daily stock data on the right. Each column records a different type of stock movement.

Column Arabic Label What to enter Editable?
A · OPENING مخزون الافتتاح Opening Stock Stock at start of day (auto-fills from yesterday's closing) MGR / Override only
GUIDE دليل المخزون Stock Guide Min | Max benchmark — shows if you're under/over stocked View only (set by demand planner)
B · WH نقل من المستودع From Warehouse Units received from JAFZA warehouse today ✓ Yes
C · STORE-IN نقل من المتجر From Another Store Units received from a sister store transfer ✓ Yes (via Transfer drawer)
D · SOLD المبيعات Units Sold Total units sold to customers today ✓ Yes
E · TRANSFER OUT نقل إلى متجر Sent to Store Units sent to another sister store today ✓ Yes (via Transfer drawer)
F · RETURN TO WH إرجاع إلى المستودع Return to Warehouse Units returned to JAFZA warehouse (write-off / damage) ✓ Yes
BALANCE الرصيد Closing Balance Auto-calculated — do not enter manually 🔒 Auto only
BALANCE = A + B + C − D − E − F
الرصيد = الافتتاح + المستودع + نقل وارد − المبيعات − نقل صادر − إرجاع
🟢
Balance turns red if it goes negative (more sold/transferred than you had in stock). This is a warning — check your entries for errors.
04
Daily Data Entry Rules قواعد إدخال البيانات اليومية
🔴
Accuracy is everything. Wrong entries affect demand planning, replenishment orders, and your store's performance KPIs. Enter carefully — double-check before saving.
Golden Rules — القواعد الذهبية
  • Enter data every day before closing — never leave it to accumulate
  • Opening stock auto-fills from yesterday's closing balance — do NOT change it unless corrected by area manager
  • Tap a cell to open the number pad. Enter the value and tap SAVE
  • Sold = units sold to paying customers only — testers and display units are NOT sold
  • If you made a mistake, tap the cell again and enter the correct value. The balance recalculates automatically
  • Do not leave a cell blank if there was actual movement — enter 0 explicitly if nothing happened
  • Once the area manager locks the day (🔒), you cannot edit entries without manager override
1
Select today's tab · اختر تبويب اليوم
The day tabs at the top show the last 5 days and the next 9 days. Today is always highlighted. Make sure you are on TODAY before entering data.
2
Tap the cell you want to edit · اضغط على الخلية التي تريد تعديلها
A number pad appears. The current value is shown. Clear it and enter your new value.
3
Tap SAVE or ADD · اضغط حفظ أو إضافة
The value saves instantly. The Balance column updates automatically. A green toast "✓ Added" confirms the save.
05
Recording Sales — SOLD Column تسجيل المبيعات — عمود المبيعات
1
Tap the red SOLD cell for the product · اضغط خلية المبيعات الحمراء للمنتج
Find the product row (scroll down or look by category). Tap the red/pink SALES cell.
2
Enter the total units sold today · أدخل إجمالي الوحدات المباعة اليوم
This is the total for the full day — not per transaction. If you sold 3 bottles of C00002 today, enter 3.
3
Tap SAVE · اضغط حفظ
The Balance updates immediately. If it turns red (negative), check that the Opening + WH + Store-In is enough to cover the sales.
⚠️
Do NOT include testers as sold. If a bottle was converted to a tester, record it in the Testers section below the main grid — not in the Sold column.
⚠️
System alert: If you try to enter more sold units than your available stock (Opening + WH + Store-In), the system shows a ⚠ EXCEEDS STOCK warning. This does not block you — but please double-check your entry before saving.
06
Transfers Between Stores النقل بين المتاجر

Transfers are tracked using a special Transfer Drawer. There are two types — transfers you receive and transfers you send.

📥 RECEIVED (Store-In — Column C)
Stock coming INTO your store from another store.
Tap the green STORE-IN cell → select the sending store → enter quantity.
📤 SENT (Transfer Out — Column E)
Stock leaving your store going to another store.
Tap the purple TRANSFER cell → select the receiving store → enter quantity.
🔴
CRITICAL RULE: When you send stock to Store B, Store B must also record the Store-In on their register for the same product, same day. Both stores must record the same transfer — it's not automatic.
1
Tap the Transfer Out or Store-In cell · اضغط خلية النقل الصادر أو الوارد
The Transfer Drawer opens showing any existing transfers for this product today.
2
Select the other store · اختر المتجر الآخر
Choose the store you are sending to (or receiving from). The dropdown shows all stores grouped by area manager.
3
Enter quantity and add a note (optional) · أدخل الكمية وأضف ملاحظة اختيارية
Type the quantity on the numpad. Add a remark if needed (e.g., "Driver: Ahmed"). Tap + ADD TRANSFER.
4
The cell shows the total + store name · الخلية تعرض الإجمالي واسم المتجر
After saving, the Transfer cell shows the quantity and the name of the other store as a chip below it. Tap the cell again to see the full breakdown.

Testers Section قسم العينات

The Tester Section appears below the main stock grid (scroll down). It tracks the status of every tester bottle in your store.

StatusArabicMeaning
🟢 ACTIVEنشطTester is on display, working, accessible to customers
🟡 LOWمنخفضTester is running low — needs refill or replacement soon
🔴 EMPTYفارغTester bottle is empty — needs replacement immediately

Tap the status pill on any tester to change its condition. The system tracks how many days each tester has been active. Empty testers should be flagged for replacement as soon as possible.
📋
Tester-to-Full conversion requests (FG requests) are submitted separately via the FG Request Form. Ask your area manager if you need to convert a full bottle into a tester.

09
Locking a Day إغلاق اليوم

At the end of each day, the area manager locks the day to prevent further editing. Once locked, the closing balance automatically carries over as the next day's Opening Stock.

What Locking Does
  • Freezes all data for that day — no one except MGR can edit it
  • Auto-carries the closing balance to tomorrow's Opening Stock column
  • Shows a 🔒 icon on the day tab
  • Generates the day summary for the manager dashboard
🔐
Manager Override: If a correction is needed after locking, the area manager can tap "Manager Override" on the locked day banner and enter their PIN to unlock editing temporarily. This is logged and audited.

Do's & Don'ts يجب وما لا يجب
✓ DO — يجب
  • Enter sales data before store closes every day
  • Double-check the date tab — make sure you're on TODAY
  • Record every product movement — WH receipts, transfers, write-offs
  • Communicate with the receiving store when doing transfers
  • Report any system errors to your area manager immediately
  • Keep your PIN private and secure
✗ DO NOT — لا يجب
  • Do NOT change Opening Stock without manager approval
  • Do NOT record tester conversions as "sold"
  • Do NOT leave multiple days blank and catch up later
  • Do NOT share your store PIN with other stores
  • Do NOT enter future dates — only enter data for today
  • Do NOT close the app mid-entry — always save first
🔴
The stock register is the official record. Discrepancies between the register and physical stock will be investigated. Accuracy protects you, your store, and your performance review.
